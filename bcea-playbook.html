BCEA Decision Framework

The playbook converts BCEA into a series of implementation decisions that can be captured, reviewed, tested, and reused across projects.

Control what mattersAvoid controlling every possible combination. Excessive granularity creates hierarchy explosion and operational frustration.
Do not finalize BCEA before COABCEA depends on COA segment structure, balancing strategy, fund hierarchy, project/grant relationships, and reporting governance.
Separate operating funds and grantsOperating funds, grants, capital projects, and internal services may require different advisory/absolute control strategies.
Design exclusions carefullyBalance sheet accounts, statistical accounts, selected sources, and certain journals may need exclusion from funds check.
Test fiscal-year behavior earlyPO rollover, carryforward, encumbrance liquidation, and year-end timing can create serious go-live risk.
Preserve evidenceEvery design decision should produce test evidence, signoff support, and reusable implementation knowledge.
