PHI / PII / SPI Safe Use

Enterprise-safe implementation guidance.

OracleToolkit is intended for implementation design, governance, configuration readiness, testing, and delivery acceleration — not production-sensitive data storage.

PIIMask names, emails, employee IDs, SSNs, addresses, and contact details before upload.
PHIDo not upload health, insurance, disability, medical, or patient-related information.
SPIDo not upload payroll, salary, tax IDs, bank data, credentials, or sensitive HR data.
Safe InputsUse sanitized SOWs, design notes, COA structures, decisions, and testing metadata.

Terms & Privacy Foundation

OracleToolkit is an implementation accelerator and methodology platform. Users are responsible for ensuring that uploaded materials are sanitized and approved for use under their client, employer, and project data governance policies.
